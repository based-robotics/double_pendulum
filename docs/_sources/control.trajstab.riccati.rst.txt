Riccati Gains Stabilization
===========================

When computing a trajectory with iLQR or Differential Dynamic Programming
(DDP), the trajectory is altered with Riccati gain
matrices. During the application these Riccati gains can be
used to stabilize the trajectory as discussed in [1].

References
----------

- [1] S. Kleff, A. Meduri, R. Budhiraja, N. Mansard, and L. Righetti, “High-
  frequency nonlinear model predictive control of a manipulator,” in 2021 IEEE
  International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA). IEEE, 2021, pp.
  7330–7336.
